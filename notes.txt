#Drag Race Memory Game

The objective of this game is to find two queens of the same. You win by finding all the pairs.

In order to play you need to:
1.Click on one of the cards. The card will flip around and the picture of a Queen will appear
2.Click on any other card. If the second card matches the first one, the two cards will stay showing the face of the Queen.
If not, they will flip back to their initial state.
